This script is designed for relocation of ERBS sites to the target subnets.
After small modification, it is also possible to rename ERBS_ to RN_ during a relacation proccess
That is an interructive script which will display menu with options.
You have to make a choice started from 1 to 6.
The subnet will be defined in the Step 1.
Step 2 will create backups and temporary files for work. 
Step 3 will delete ERBS sites from OSS topology database.
Step 4 will create ERBS under target subnet.
Step 5 will show you that readme.txt file
Step 6 will perform exit from the script\program.
